Mobile rental vehicles in Wellington city.
Mobile Scooters

There are currently 2 mobile scooters in Wellington, Jump by Uber and Flamingo. In order to find and rent these electric scooters, you need to install their apps on your phone and find them on the map. Once you've found them, you can unlock the vehicle via app by scanning the QR code. They charge for the vehicle in-app as well. Linked to Flamingo website.
